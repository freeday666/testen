Community Rules

  1. Be Respectful: Treat all members with kindness and respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination will be tolerated.
  2. No Spamming: Avoid spamming messages, emojis, or links. Keep conversations relevant and on-topic.
  3. Use Appropriate Language: Maintain a friendly environment. Refrain from using offensive or NSFW language.
  4. No Toxic Behavior: Do not bullying, trolling, or provoking others. Keep discussions constructive.
  5. Follow Discord's Terms of Service: Abide by Discord's official rules and policies at all times.
  6. Respect Privacy: Do not share personal information of yourself or others without consent.
  7. No Self-Promotion or Advertising: Avoid unsolicited promotions, links, or advertisements unless permitted.
  8. Use Correct Channels: Post content in the appropriate channels. Off-topic posts may be removed.
  9. No NSFW Content: Keep all content safe for work. NSFW images, videos, or discussions are prohibited.
  10. Follow Staff Instructions: Respect and comply with moderators' and staff's guidance and decisions.
  11. No Illegal Activities: Discussions or actions involving illegal activities are strictly forbidden.
  12. Report Issues: If you encounter problems or rule-breaking, report to staff rather than confronting members.
  13. Have Fun, but Keep It Safe: Enjoy your time here while respecting community guidelines.
  14. No Impersonation: Do not impersonate other members, staff, or public figures.
  15. Language Restrictions: Use designated language channels if available.
  16. Respect Intellectual Property: Do not share copyrighted content without permission.
  17. No Hate Speech or Discrimination: This community is inclusive. Hate speech of any kind is unacceptable.
  18. Stay on Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the community’s purpose.
  19. Avoid Sensitive Topics: Be mindful of discussions around politics, religion, or divisive issues.
  20. Have a Positive Attitude: Contribute to a welcoming atmosphere and encourage others.
